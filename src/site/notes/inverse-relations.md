### Inverse Relations
Let $R$ be a [[relations|relation]]. The inverse of $R$ is obtained by reversing each pair of ordered numbers inside the relation, and is denoted with $R^{-1}$.
$$
R^{-1}=\{(y,x)|(x,y)\in R\}
$$

### Movement on a Curve
The magnitud of the speed function is defined as:
$$
|v(t)|=\sqrt{(f'(t))^2+(g'(t))^2+(h'(t))^2}
$$
And the length of the arch of the curve, from point $a$ to point $b$ is:
$$
L=\int_a^b |v(t)|dt
$$

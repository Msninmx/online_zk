### Cartesian Product
Let $A$ and $B$ be two non empty [[set-union|sets]]. The Cartesian Product of these sets is the set of ordered pairs, which in turn, becomes a [[relations|relation]].
$$
A \times B=\{ (a,b)|a \in A \land b \in B \}
$$
